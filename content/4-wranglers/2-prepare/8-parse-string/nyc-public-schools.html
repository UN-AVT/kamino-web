Let’s take a look at the column Street.Address. Both the Street Name and Building number are in the column. Perhaps, we’ll like to sepearate them.

head(df$Street.Address)
## [1] "425 West 33rd Street" "650 Academy Street"   "111 Columbia Street" 
## [4] "350 Grand Street"     "350 Grand Street"     "350 Grand Street"
df2 <- data.frame(Building_Number = rep(1,435))
df2$Building_Number <- parse_number(df$Street.Address)
df2$Street_Name <- gsub('[[:digit:]]+', '', df$Street.Address)
df2
