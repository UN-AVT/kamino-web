Kamino

Data Science for Development

Kamino is a free and open community-developed data science learning program specifically designed to promote the skills and capabilities to take on the toughest analytical challenges in sustainable development.

The program is focused on real-life application for solving problems using hundreds of data sets and cases to illustrate the use of methods.

Skills to make an impact

Kamino is a process and task focused approach to gain the knowledge, skills, and abilities to apply methods for the entire data analysis and visualization lifecycle. The process is covered by 16 activities which each goal, task, methods, and solutions explained in full detail.
